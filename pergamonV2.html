Benchmark Protocol

A proven protocol developed with legal experts (15+ years experience) specialized in European business law.

01

Case-Based Exercises

Lawyers craft exercises with gold answers directly reflecting real-world client cases to ensure practical relevance and complexity.

02

Evaluation Rubrics

Detailed gold answers and rubrics are developed to facilitate precise and consistent evaluation of AI responses.

03

Expert Review

Each case question and rubric is reviewed by a second expert. On average, 10 hours are invested per exercise.

04

Dual Evaluation

LLM-as-Judge criteria combined with deterministic verifiers against official legal databases like Légifrance and Judilibre.
